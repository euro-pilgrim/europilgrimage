About

Hi, Enda here - I'm planning on walking around Europe on ancient pilgrim paths to visit the shrines of the 'European Marian Network'

I'll live simply and stay at campsites or pilgrim shelters. As I walk along I like to enjoy Nature and dedicate the pilgrimage to all those in ill-health.
